Who is Olivia Rodrigo?

Olivia Rodrigo was born February 20, 2003 in Murrieta, California, U.S. She is an American singer, songwriter.She first gained popularity with a role on the television show High School Musical: The Musical—The Series (2019–23) and who later launched a successful music career..

Album Cover

The GUTS album cover, photographed by Larissa Hofmann, features Olivia Rodrigo reclining on a purple floorHer nails are painted with chipped black nail polish. The dominant purple hue continues the color theme from her debut album, SOUR, but in a darker shade, reflecting a more mature and introspective tone. The visual elements—such as the chipped nails and moody palette—convey a grunge-inspired aesthetic, aligning with the album's themes of vulnerability and emotional turbulence. ​
